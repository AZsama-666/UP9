MEMBERSHIP

Membership System

UP9 provides three participation paths: Maker | Partner | Host. You can hold multiple roles simultaneously; we only record current roles without rigid metrics.

Maker | Create & Contribute

What you can do: Post topics or mini-courses; provide templates/checklists; share tools; write weekly reviews; answer questions or make referrals.

Benefits: Name display; content archive; weekly newsletter mention; access to collaboration group
How to Join: Submit a recent contribution (link or screenshot), get tagged as Maker after approval. Pause and return anytime

Contributor | Subscribe & Support

Gain access and support through service packages (no returns promised).

Core: Public event seats; basic resource access; open day participation
Pro: Priority seats in private sessions/topic discussions; monthly targeted matching
Elite: Monthly 1-on-1 consultation; material review (pitch decks/proposals)
Fellow: Co-hosting/sponsorship; joint brand releases

Host | Event Organizer

What you can do: Organize small gatherings (topic discussions/workshops/open office/demos/local meetups); publish agenda; provide key takeaways within 48 hours after event.

Benefits: Official website & newsletter exposure; host resource pack (process/templates/scripts); partnership connections; event funding guidance when needed
How to Join: Host a trial session first, submit review, continue after mutual confirmation
Note: No rigid metrics; consensus on "rhythm and review"
